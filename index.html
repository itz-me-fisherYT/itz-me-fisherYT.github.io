Lifedupey

Lifedupey is a Lifesteal Dupe Minecraft server. On this server, when you kill another player, you steal their health, making every PvP fight high-stakes. Additionally, the server allows item duplication (duping), so players can copy rare items like enchanted gear or diamonds, creating fast-paced, chaotic gameplay. Essentially, it's a PvP-heavy server where both health and items can be stolen or duplicated for intense action.
